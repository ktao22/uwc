FAQ

Timeline & Details of Summer Camp

UWC Summer Camp in Neuroscience Research will be held online during June 23 - July 11, 2025. Only UWC students can apply.

Do I need experience in neuroscience to join the camp?

No, we are looking for students who are passionate and motivated about learning neuroscience, you do not necessarily need prior experience. However, taking relevant courses like IB sciences might be helpful to understand further content.

Journal Submissions

Open on July 20, the same time program students are done with their research papers. Any substantial research from a UWCer can be submitted for review in the journal, in the current fields available.

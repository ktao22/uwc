FAQ

2025 Conference Dates

June 23th - 28th, 2025

Do I need prior experience in research to join the mentorship program?

No, we are looking for students who are passionate and motivated about learning an advanced field, you do not necessarily need prior experience. We are here to guide you.

What is the UWC Science Research Journal?

Multimedia platform to share the UWC community's work in science, whether it be a research article, explainer video, review. Articles will comply with the rigorous standards and practices of research with our Editorial Board, while science communication projects will be evaluated by relevant editors for merit of being featured.

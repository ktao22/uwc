UWC Short Course in Neuroscience Research

Three-week virtual research program specialising in neuroscience, built to equip students in gaining skills in research design, data collection, and presentation of findings.


Dive into one week of research skills. After that, one week of neuroscience foundations. Then, one week of your very own collaborative group research project paired with mentorship and guidance from UWC alumni. Graduate from the Capstone Project with a stunning review paper to present to peers and invited academics at the very 1st Virtual UWC Neuroscience Conference.

2025 Program Dates: June 23rd - July 11th

Applications are only open to current UWC students who are entering or enrolled in their 1st year at one of the 18 campuses. Signups close 1st June, 2025.
